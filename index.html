Introduction to Algorithms

Authored by Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, and Clifford Stein, this textbook is a good toolbox for my projects whether they are academic or personal. Hence, I enjoy understanding the proofs, and then implement the algorithms of this textbook on computer algorithms.

The Art of Computer Programming

Authored by Donald Knuth, this textbook is more theoretical than Introduction to Algorithms. It would be a major milestone in my understanding of Computer Science. Hence, I plan to acquire it in the near future. Indeed, this textbook will allow me to delve deeper into a more mathematical and theoretical way of studying Computer Science.
