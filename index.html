The first-year courses are focused on mathematics and computer science. This leads to a general knowledge that allows understanding a diverse range of problems in order to better solve them.

Futhermore, our Soft Skills Center profides courses on professional and human aspects. Indeed, our end goal is to work as a team with our partners whether it is in the professional world or in the associative world.

  • Signal Processing and Control Systems

Introduction to Algorithms

Authored by Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, and Clifford Stein, this textbook is a good tool box for my projects whether they are academic or personnal. Moreover, I enjoy to understand the proofs, and then implement the algorithms of this textbook on computer algorithms.

The Art of Computer Programming

Authored by Donald Knuth, this textbook is more theorical than Introduction to Algorithms. It would be a major milestone in my understanding of Computer Science. Hence, I plan to acquire it in the near future. Indeed, this textbook will allow me to delve deeper into a more mathematical and theorical way of studying Computer Science.
